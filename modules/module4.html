Lighting Effects Simulation (Fake Demo)

1. Oblique Lighting

Oblique lighting highlights texture by casting long shadows from the side. Drag your mouse over the canvas to "move the light".

2. Diffused Lighting

Diffused lighting spreads evenly, reducing shadows and flattening texture. Hover to see the effect soften.

3. Transmitted Lighting

Transmitted lighting passes through the object, emphasizing translucency (like a leaf). Click to toggle glow effect.
